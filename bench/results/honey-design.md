## Honest limits

- **7 tasks, 3 runs.** Enough to show a consistent effect, not a leaderboard. Judge panels overlap, so the small quality edge over honey is within noise — the defensible claim is **equal-or-better quality at materially fewer tokens**. The token win (−19% vs baseline, beating honey's −9%) is the robust finding.
- **Single model** (`claude-opus-4-8`). Re-run with `MODEL=…` to check portability.
- honey's earlier user-facing **+12%** came from a 3-task set over-weighted to `landing-page`; on this broader set honey is −9%, and honey-design recovers the polish-token premium that remained.
